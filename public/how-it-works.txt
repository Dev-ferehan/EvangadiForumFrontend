<h3>How it Works</h3>
<h4>Welcome to our community!</h4>
    <p>To start exchanging knowledge and getting answers to your questions, follow these simple steps:</p>
    <hr>
    <h4>1. Create an Account (Sign Up)</h4>
    <p>First, join our family by creating a personal account on the website. This allows you to ask questions and provide answers to others.</p>
    <h4>2. Ask a Question</h4>
    <p>Stuck on a problem? Use the "Ask Question" page to submit your inquiry.</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Title:</strong> Give your question a brief, descriptive title.</li>
        <li><strong>Description:</strong> Write a detailed explanation of what you need help with.</li>
        <li><strong>Tags:</strong> Add keywords to categorize your question (e.g., JavaScript, React) so the right experts can find it quickly.</li>
    </ul>
    <h4>3. Learn from Answers</h4>
    <p>Once you post your question, knowledgeable members of our community will provide responses. You can read through these answers to find the best solution for your problem.</p>
    <h4>4. Help Others (Post an Answer)</h4>
    <p>If you see a question about a topic you are familiar with, help out by sharing your expertise and experience. You can use the "Post Answer" section to submit your response.</p>
    <h4>5. Share Knowledge</h4>
    <p>This platform belongs to all of us. By participating in the Q&A process, we learn from each other and solve problems together.</p>
    <div class="important-notes">
        <h4>Important Notes:</h4>
        <p>Always ensure your questions and answers are clear, respectful, and helpful to the community.</p>
        <p><strong>For your security, never share your account password with anyone.</strong></p>
    </div>
